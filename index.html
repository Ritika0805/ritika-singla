About

I'm a QA Lead and Test Automation Architect with 14+ years building quality into complex distributed systems across logistics, financial data, and enterprise SaaS. At Maersk, I own end-to-end quality for microservices on AWS — designing Selenium 4 + Java frameworks from scratch, embedding them as quality gates in CI/CD, and validating event-driven services from UI through APIs, async workflows (Temporal, Camunda), and Kafka pipelines.

My core strength is automation architecture that lasts: maintainable frameworks, service virtualization with WireMock and Microcks, contract testing, and observability-driven root-cause analysis with Grafana/Loki. I test the system, not just the UI — because bad data is the most expensive bug.

Earlier, I led test strategy for enterprise clients at IBM and spent seven years scaling QA across financial-data platforms at IHS Markit (now S&P Global). I'm currently deepening my work in Playwright and AI-augmented testing, and exploring QA Lead, QA Manager, and SDET Lead opportunities (remote-friendly).
